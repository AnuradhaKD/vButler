## User Story

**As a** hotel guest completing my pre-arrival checklist,
**I want** to review and acknowledge the hotel's Terms & Conditions and Payment Policy,
**So that** I understand my obligations as a guest and Browns Hotels & Resorts has a digital record of my acceptance.

---

## Background / Context

Formal acceptance of hotel Terms & Conditions and Payment Policy is a legal and operational requirement for the pre-arrival process. Capturing this acceptance digitally — with a timestamp — provides a defensible record for the property and replaces the need for paper forms or verbal confirmations at check-in. The T&C summary approach (with an expandable full text) is designed to ensure guests can genuinely read the content without being forced to scroll through a lengthy document, supporting informed consent.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The Terms & Conditions form is rendered within its expandable accordion card (US-05.1)
- [ ] A T&C summary block is displayed, sourced from `chain.json → termsAndConditions.summary`; this is a short paragraph (3-5 sentences) of the key terms
- [ ] A "Read full Terms & Conditions" expanding accordion link is displayed below the summary
- [ ] Tapping the "Read full Terms" link expands an inline text block showing the full T&C content from `chain.json → termsAndConditions.fullText`
- [ ] The expanding full text block collapses when the link is tapped again (toggle behaviour)
- [ ] A mandatory checkbox is displayed with the label: "I have read and accept the Hotel Terms & Conditions and Payment Policy"
- [ ] The checkbox must be ticked before the "Confirm Acceptance" button becomes enabled
- [ ] When the checkbox is unticked, the "Confirm Acceptance" button is visually disabled (`opacity: 0.5`, `cursor: not-allowed`) and does not respond to taps
- [ ] On tapping "Confirm Acceptance" with the checkbox ticked:
  - Acceptance is stored: `vb:preArrival:{resvId}.tcsAccepted = { complete: true, accepted: true, timestamp: Date.now() }`
  - The checklist item is marked complete
  - The inline summary in the collapsed card reads: "Accepted on [DD MMM YYYY HH:MM]"
- [ ] The item cannot be un-completed once accepted (no "Remove" or reset option — acceptance is final for the reservation)
- [ ] Previously accepted state is restored on return; the card shows completed state immediately without re-presenting the checkbox

## Technical Notes

- **Alpine.js component**: `x-data="termsForm()"` embedded in the US-05.1 accordion card
- **Data properties**: `isChecked: false`, `showFullText: false`, `isComplete: false`, `acceptedTimestamp: null`
- **chain.json structure**:
  ```json
  {
    "termsAndConditions": {
      "summary": "By confirming your reservation...",
      "fullText": "Full legal text..."
    }
  }
  ```
- **Checkbox binding**: `:checked="isChecked"` on hidden input; `@change="isChecked = $event.target.checked"` on label/input; button `:disabled="!isChecked"` with `:class="{ 'opacity-50 pointer-events-none': !isChecked }"`
- **Save**:
  ```js
  confirm() {
    if (!this.isChecked) return;
    const timestamp = Date.now();
    const preArrival = StorageService.get(`vb:preArrival:${resvId}`) || {};
    preArrival.tcsAccepted = {
      complete: true,
      accepted: true,
      timestamp,
      summary: `Accepted on ${dayjs(timestamp).format('DD MMM YYYY HH:mm')}`
    };
    StorageService.set(`vb:preArrival:${resvId}`, preArrival);
    this.isComplete = true;
    this.$dispatch('preArrival:itemComplete', { item: 'tcsAccepted' });
  }
  ```
- **Restore on load**:
  ```js
  const saved = StorageService.get(`vb:preArrival:${resvId}`)?.tcsAccepted;
  if (saved?.complete) { this.isComplete = true; this.acceptedTimestamp = saved.timestamp; }
  ```
- **No undo**: once `tcsAccepted.complete = true`, no UI path allows resetting to incomplete

## Test Cases

### Happy Path

1. Open the Terms & Conditions accordion card
2. Verify T&C summary paragraph is displayed
3. Tap "Read full Terms & Conditions"; verify full text expands
4. Tap again; verify full text collapses
5. Verify "Confirm Acceptance" button is disabled (greyed out)
6. Tick the checkbox; verify button becomes active (full opacity)
7. Tap "Confirm Acceptance"; verify card collapses with "Accepted on [DD MMM YYYY HH:MM]"
8. Verify `vb:preArrival:{resvId}.tcsAccepted.accepted = true` and timestamp stored
9. Reload page; verify T&C item still shows completed state with original acceptance timestamp

### Edge Cases

- Tapping "Confirm Acceptance" without ticking checkbox: button is disabled, no action occurs
- Returning after previous acceptance: card immediately shows completed state; accordion body shows read-only confirmation, not the checkbox form
- `chain.json` missing `termsAndConditions.summary`: show fallback text "Please review our standard hotel terms and payment policies at check-in"
- `chain.json` missing `termsAndConditions.fullText`: "Read full Terms" link is hidden if no full text is available
- Full T&C text is very long (5000+ chars): scrollable container handles it within 200px max-height

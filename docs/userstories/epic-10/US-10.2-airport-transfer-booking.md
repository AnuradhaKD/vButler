## User Story

**As a** hotel guest,
**I want** to book an airport transfer for arrival or departure directly from the app, including my flight details and vehicle preference,
**So that** I have a confirmed, stress-free transfer arranged without having to call the hotel or use a third-party service.

---

## Background / Context

Airport transfers are the most high-stakes transport interaction for hotel guests тАФ a missed or disorganised transfer at arrival or departure creates immediate dissatisfaction. Browns Hotels & Resorts properties in Sri Lanka handle transfers to Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) and domestic airports. A well-designed booking form that captures flight number, arrival/departure time, passenger count, and vehicle preference gives the hotel operations team everything they need to execute flawlessly, while giving the guest confidence through a confirmation reference and a proactive WhatsApp follow-up promise.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The Airport Transfer booking screen is reachable from the Transport Hub "Airport Transfer" card at route `#/transport/airport-transfer`
- [ ] A direction toggle is displayed at the top: "Arrival" and "Departure"; selecting one changes the form fields shown
- [ ] **Arrival form fields**: Flight Number (text input, e.g., UL 225), Arrival Date (Flatpickr, from today onwards), Arrival Time (time picker, HH:MM), Number of Passengers (stepper 1тАУ8)
- [ ] **Departure form fields**: Departure Date (Flatpickr, from today to check-out date), Desired Hotel Departure Time (time picker), Flight Number (text input), Number of Passengers (stepper 1тАУ8)
- [ ] A vehicle selection section displays vehicle option cards loaded from `transport.option.vehicles[]` where `option.type === "airport_transfer"`; each card shows: vehicle icon, vehicle label (e.g., "Sedan", "SUV", "Minivan"), passenger capacity, and price (LKR) for the transfer
- [ ] Exactly one vehicle must be selected before submission; the selected vehicle card has a brand primary border + checkmark badge
- [ ] A special instructions text field (optional, max 300 chars) is present
- [ ] "Confirm Transfer" button is disabled until all required fields are filled and a vehicle is selected
- [ ] On confirm: a request is written to `vb:requests[]` with `category: "transport"`, `type: "airport_transfer"`, direction, all flight/date/time details, vehicle selection, and passenger count
- [ ] A success confirmation screen (replacing the form) displays: direction, flight number, date/time, vehicle type, passenger count, reference number (`TRF-{timestamp}`), and the message "We'll confirm your transfer by WhatsApp 24 hours before your travel time"
- [ ] The folio is charged the vehicle price via `FolioService.addCharge`
- [ ] Screen renders correctly in dark mode

## UI / UX Notes

- Direction toggle: full-width 2-option pill toggle at top; "Arrival" default selected; switching animates form field changes via Alpine `x-show` with transition
- Arrival time: time picker (Flatpickr enableTime mode) for arrival time at airport тАФ used to calculate pickup timing; labelled "Estimated arrival time at airport"
- Departure: "Desired Hotel Departure Time" is when the vehicle should arrive at the hotel; labelled clearly to avoid confusion with flight departure time
- Passenger stepper: centred layout, large 44 px tap targets, count in 18 px bold; matches styling used in other forms
- Vehicle cards: 2-column grid on mobile; each card 100 px height; vehicle SVG icon (64 px) top-centre; label (14 px semi-bold); "Up to X passengers" (12 px muted); price (LKR X,XXX in brand primary) bottom; selected state: brand primary border (2 px), white checkmark badge on top-right corner
- WhatsApp confirmation note: small info card with WhatsApp green icon, italicised note text (13 px)
- Success screen: full-width brand gradient top banner; "Transfer Confirmed" in Playfair Display; details in a summary card; reference in monospace 16 px bold

## Technical Notes

- Route: `#/transport/airport-transfer`
- Alpine.js component: `x-data="airportTransfer()"`:
  - `direction: 'arrival'` тАФ toggle state
  - `flightNumber`, `travelDate`, `travelTime`, `passengerCount`, `selectedVehicleId`, `specialInstructions`
  - `vehicles` тАФ loaded from `DataService` filtering `transport.options` for `type === "airport_transfer"` and extracting `.vehicles[]`
  - `isValid` тАФ computed: all required fields filled and `selectedVehicleId` set
  - `selectedVehicle` тАФ computed: lookup vehicle by `selectedVehicleId`
- Departure date Flatpickr: `maxDate: vb:session.checkOut`; Arrival date: no max constraint
- Reference number: `"TRF-" + Date.now()`
- Folio charge: `FolioService.addCharge({ description: "Airport Transfer - " + selectedVehicle.label, amount: selectedVehicle.price, ref: requestId })`
- Request schema:
  ```json
  {
    "id": "TRF-1700000000000",
    "category": "transport",
    "type": "airport_transfer",
    "direction": "arrival",
    "flightNumber": "UL 225",
    "travelDate": "2026-03-16",
    "travelTime": "14:30",
    "passengerCount": 2,
    "vehicleId": "vehicle-sedan",
    "vehicleLabel": "Sedan",
    "vehiclePrice": 8500,
    "specialInstructions": "One large suitcase",
    "status": "confirmed",
    "createdAt": "2026-03-15T10:00:00Z"
  }
  ```

## Test Cases

### Happy Path

1. Guest taps "Airport Transfer" on Transport Hub тЖТ booking screen loads; "Arrival" selected by default
2. Guest enters: Flight UL 225, Arrival Date 16 March, Arrival Time 14:30, 2 passengers
3. Vehicle cards shown: Sedan (2 pax, LKR 8,500), SUV (4 pax, LKR 12,000), Minivan (7 pax, LKR 18,000)
4. Guest selects Sedan тЖТ card gets border + checkmark
5. Taps "Confirm Transfer" тЖТ success screen: "Arrival ┬╖ UL 225 ┬╖ 16 Mar 2026 ┬╖ 14:30 ┬╖ Sedan ┬╖ 2 pax ┬╖ TRF-XXXX ┬╖ We'll confirm by WhatsApp 24 hrs before"
6. Folio charge LKR 8,500 added

7. Guest switches to "Departure": form changes to Departure Date, Hotel Departure Time, Flight Number, passengers; validation and submission work identically

### Edge Cases

- **Departure date beyond check-out**: Flatpickr disables dates after check-out
- **Flight number left blank**: Form validation blocks submission; "Flight Number is required" error shown
- **No vehicles configured**: "No vehicle options available тАФ please contact the concierge" message shown
- **Single vehicle configured**: Only one card shown; automatically selected on load
- **Passenger count at 8**: "+" button disabled
- **Special instructions at 300 char limit**: Counter shows "300/300"; input blocked
- **Dark mode**: Toggle, vehicle cards, selected state, success screen all verified

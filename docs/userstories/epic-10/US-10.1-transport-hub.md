## User Story

**As a** hotel guest,
**I want** to see all transport options available through the hotel in one place and quickly access the relevant booking form,
**So that** I can arrange travel seamlessly without searching for concierge assistance or third-party apps.

---

## Background / Context

Guests at Browns Hotels & Resorts properties frequently need transport — airport transfers on arrival or departure, shuttles to nearby attractions, car hire for day trips, and local tuk-tuks or taxis. Consolidating all transport options into a single hub with clear pricing indicators and inline booking forms removes the need to call the concierge for routine arrangements and positions Destinity vButler as the guest's primary service interface throughout their stay.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The Transport Hub is accessible from the Home Quick Actions grid via a "Transport" tile
- [ ] Up to 4 full-width transport type cards are displayed, corresponding to configured options in `property.json → transport.options[]`
- [ ] The following transport types are supported: Airport Transfer, Hotel Shuttle, Car Hire, Local Transport (each identified by `option.type`)
- [ ] Each card displays: a type-specific icon (SVG), card title, short description, and a "From LKR X,XXX" price indicator derived from `option.basePrice`
- [ ] If an option is not configured for the property (absent from `transport.options[]`), its card is not shown
- [ ] Tapping a transport card expands an inline booking form below the card (Alpine.js `x-show` toggle); tapping again collapses it
- [ ] Only one booking form can be expanded at a time; expanding one card collapses any previously open form
- [ ] The inline booking form for each transport type is the abbreviated entry form for that type (full forms in US-10.2, US-10.3, US-10.4); alternatively, tapping the card navigates to the full booking screen if the form is complex
- [ ] A "My Upcoming Transfers" section is displayed at the bottom of the Hub, showing future transport requests from `vb:requests[]` with `category: "transport"` and a date in the future
- [ ] If no upcoming transfers exist, the section is hidden
- [ ] Screen renders correctly in dark mode

## UI / UX Notes

- Page header: "Transport & Transfers" in Playfair Display 22 px; subtext "Arrange travel with ease" in 13 px muted
- Transport cards: full-width, 80 px height, white background (dark: surface-card), 12 px radius, 16 px padding; icon left (40 px), title + description centre-left, price pill right ("From LKR X,XXX" in brand accent pill)
- Card icons: plane for Airport Transfer, bus for Hotel Shuttle, car for Car Hire, tuk-tuk / map-pin for Local Transport
- Expanded booking form: slides down from card bottom with `max-height` CSS transition; brand primary left border (3 px) on expanded form container
- "My Upcoming Transfers" section: card list with transport type icon, destination/description, date/time, status badge (Confirmed / Pending)
- Price indicator: "From LKR X,XXX" — formatted with `formatLKR`; if no base price, show "Price on request"

## Technical Notes

- Route: `#/transport`
- Alpine.js component: `x-data="transportHub()"`:
  - `options` — loaded from `DataService.getProperty().transport.options`
  - `activeOptionId: null` — ID of currently expanded card
  - `upcomingTransfers` — filtered from `StorageService.getRequests({ category: 'transport' })` where request date > today
  - `toggleOption(id)` — sets `activeOptionId = (activeOptionId === id ? null : id)`
- Inline forms for Airport Transfer and Car Hire link to full booking screens (US-10.2, US-10.4) rather than rendering inline (due to complexity)
- Hotel Shuttle and Local Transport have simple enough forms to render inline
- Card `option.type` values: `"airport_transfer"`, `"shuttle"`, `"car_hire"`, `"local_transport"`

## Test Cases

### Happy Path

1. Guest taps "Transport" in Quick Actions
2. Transport Hub loads with 4 cards: Airport Transfer (From LKR 8,500), Hotel Shuttle (Complimentary), Car Hire (From LKR 12,000), Local Transport (From LKR 2,500)
3. Guest taps "Hotel Shuttle" → inline shuttle booking form expands below the card
4. Guest taps "Airport Transfer" → navigates to full Airport Transfer booking screen (US-10.2)
5. My Upcoming Transfers section shows 1 confirmed airport transfer for tomorrow

### Edge Cases

- **Only 2 transport options configured**: Only 2 cards shown; no empty card placeholders
- **No upcoming transfers**: Section hidden; no layout gap
- **Two cards tapped in sequence**: First collapses before second expands
- **Dark mode**: Cards, inline form, upcoming transfers section verified

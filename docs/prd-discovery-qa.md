# Destinity vButler PRD — Discovery Q&A

Questions asked during PRD discovery session before writing the Product Requirements Document.

**Date:** 2026-03-15

---

**Q1: The designs reference "Lumina Reserves" as the hotel/brand name. Is this the actual brand name to use in the PRD, or is this just a placeholder used in the mockups? What is the real product/brand name?**

> Use main hotel chain name as Browns Hotels and Resorts. Hotel name is Newburge Ella.

---

**Q2: The app supports multiple reservations across multiple properties in the same chain. When a guest logs in, should they see all their reservations (past, current, upcoming) across all Browns properties — or only reservations for the property they're currently checked in to?**

> Want to show all reservations across all Browns properties.

---

**Q3: What platforms should this app target? iOS only, Android only, both native apps, or a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on mobile browsers?**

> This can use web browser — looks like an app in mobile browsers, but on desktops and laptops it looks like a normal website.

---

**Q4: For authentication, the designs show a phone number + OTP flow. Is that the only login method, or should guests also be able to log in with email, social login (Google/Apple), or a reservation confirmation number?**

> No, just email and password then OTP.

---

**Q5: For services (room service, laundry, transport, wake-up call, room makeup, etc.) — when a guest places a request through the app, where does it go? Does it connect to an existing hotel system/PMS (like Opera, Mews, etc.), or does it just notify hotel staff via email/SMS/dashboard?**

> No, it is connected to the existing PMS and also goes as a WhatsApp message to their hotel internal groups.

---

**Q6: The designs show an Invoices / Financial Hub screen. Can guests actually pay outstanding balances through the app, or is this screen read-only (just viewing their bill/charges)?**

> Yes, guests can pay through the app. Advance payment is also allowed.

---

**Q7: For the Curated Experiences & Places to Visit sections — is this content managed by hotel staff (they add/edit local experiences and activities), or is it static content hardcoded per property?**

> Yes, these details are added by hotel staff. All hotels' unique things must be added by hotel staff, and also main chain things like theme colour and logos etc.

---

**Q8: What languages should the app support? English only, or multilingual? If multilingual, which languages are priority for Browns Hotels & Resorts guests?**

> Mainly English, else Sinhala, Tamil, Arabic, and German also. It can be changed by dropdown.

---

**Q9: For guest complaints/feedback — the designs show a complaint submission form. Should complaints have a tracking status (submitted → in progress → resolved) visible to the guest, or is it just a one-way submission with no status updates?**

> Yes, guest can track the complaint.

---

**Q10: What is the primary goal / success metric for this app from a business perspective? For example: reduce front desk call volume, increase upsell revenue (experiences, services), improve guest satisfaction scores (NPS/TripAdvisor), or all of the above?**

> All of the above.

---

**Q11: Is there a specific launch timeline or phase in mind? For example, is there an MVP phase (core features first) followed by later phases — or should the PRD cover the full product vision all at once?**

> PRD should cover the full product vision.

---

*Discovery session conducted: 2026-03-15*

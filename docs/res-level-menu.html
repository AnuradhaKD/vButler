16. Feedback

Sections (emoji ratings 😞😕😐🙂😄)

  • Hotel Services (11 items, max 55 pts)
  • Room & Facilities (8 items, max 40 pts)
  • Associates (4 items, max 20 pts)
  • Food & Beverage (7 items, max 35 pts)

Scoring Logic

  • Score > 100 → TripAdvisor / Google review redirect
  • Score < 60 → GM escalation message
  • One submission per reservation
✅ In-House: mid-stay feedback ✅ Checked-Out: post-stay feedback
